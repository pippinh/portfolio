Nirvana

Nirvana redefined the music industry in the early '90s with their raw sound and emotional depth. Their breakthrough album, Nevermind, led the grunge movement and influenced generations of musicians.

With hits like "Smells Like Teen Spirit" and "Come as You Are," their music continues to resonate. Despite their short-lived career, Nirvana's legacy remains timeless.

Sonic Youth

Sonic Youth pushed the boundaries of alternative rock with their experimental guitar tunings and avant-garde sound. Their influence on the indie and grunge scenes is immeasurable.

Albums like Daydream Nation and Goo brought them widespread recognition, while tracks such as "Teen Age Riot" and "Kool Thing" became underground anthems.

Stone Temple Pilots

Stone Temple Pilots blended grunge, psychedelic rock, and alternative influences to create a unique, powerful sound. Their album Core launched them into mainstream success.

With tracks like "Plush," "Interstate Love Song," and "Vasoline," STP became one of the defining bands of the 1990s, leaving an enduring mark on rock music.
